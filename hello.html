Citrus Fruits and Leaves Disease Detection

Citrus fruits and leaves diseases are major threats to agricultural productivity, and accurate and efficient automated detection methods are a must.

Developed an automated system to detect diseases in citrus fruits and leaves using MobileNetV2. Built a Flask-based web app for real-time image upload and disease classification. Classified images into canker, black spot, greening, and healthy categories. Improved model accuracy with preprocessing and data augmentation techniques. Enabled fast and accessible disease diagnosis to support timely agricultural action.

Media Stream Analytics

Designed and implemented a real-time analytics pipeline for a media streaming platform to monitor viewership, ad revenue, and demographics. Ingested streaming logs via Kinesis, processed and transformed data using EMR and Glue, and automated ETL workflows with Lambda and Airflow (MWAA). Stored outputs in Snowflake and S3 with incremental updates via Iceberg tables, and built QuickSight dashboards for actionable insights, reducing data latency from hours to minutes.
